📋 Step-by-Step Claim Guide
1
Within 72 Hours — Intimate immediately
Call PMFBY helpline 14447 or your insurance company. Take photos and videos of damaged crop with timestamp. Note the date and time of damage event.
2
Day 1–3 — File formal intimation
Visit nearest CSC, bank branch, or file online at pmfby.gov.in/claimIntimation. Keep your policy number and Aadhaar ready. Get acknowledgement receipt.
3
Day 3–7 — Submit written application
Submit written claim application with photos to your bank or insurance company. Keep photocopies of all documents submitted.
4
Week 2 — Crop Cutting Experiment (CCE)
Government officials will conduct CCE on your field. Be present on your field during this. CCE determines actual yield loss for claim calculation.
5
After CCE — Claim disbursement
Claim amount calculated based on yield loss vs threshold yield. Amount credited directly to your Aadhaar-linked bank account within 2–3 weeks of CCE.
